Privacy Policy

1. Purpose of the Policy

This privacy policy outlines how StudySphere collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) in South Africa. The policy applies to all clients (learners and their guardians) and any personal data that is collected during the provision of tutoring services.

2. Definitions

Personal Information: Any information that can be used to identify a person, such as name, contact details, address, academic records, or any other data related to the learner or their family. Processing: Any operation concerning personal information, including collection, storage, use, or dissemination.

3. Collection of Personal Information

3.1 Types of Information Collected

Contact Information: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and physical addresses of learners and their guardians. Educational Information: Academic records, grades, assignments, and feedback related to the learner’s educational progress. Special Categories of Personal Data: Any sensitive information such as medical conditions or special educational needs that may be relevant for tutoring services.

3.2 How Information is Collected

Personal information is collected directly from learners or their guardians through forms, email, phone conversations, or during tutoring sessions. With the consent of the learner or guardian, additional academic records may be obtained from schools or educational institutions if necessary.

4. Purpose of Collecting Personal Information

The personal information collected by StudySphere is used for the following purposes: Tutoring Services: To design and deliver personalized learning plans, assess learner progress, and adjust teaching methods as necessary. Communication: To communicate with learners and their guardians regarding tutoring sessions, progress reports, scheduling, and billing. Legal Compliance: To comply with any legal obligations, including reporting in cases of abuse, neglect, or safety concerns under child protection laws.

5. Consent to Collect and Use Personal Information

StudySphere will obtain explicit consent from learners or their guardians before collecting any personal information. Consent may be withdrawn at any time, and the tutor will stop processing personal information unless required by law. In cases involving minors, consent must be provided by a parent or legal guardian.

6. Storage of Personal Information

6.1 How Information is Stored

Personal information is stored securely, whether in physical form (e.g., paper records) or electronically (e.g., on a computer or cloud storage). Electronic records are protected by passwords, encryption, and other security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or theft.

6.2 Retention Period

Personal information will only be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including fulfilling legal and accounting obligations. Once the information is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or destroyed.

7. Disclosure of Personal Information

7.1 Third Parties

StudySphere does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared with third parties in the following cases: Service Providers: Personal information may be shared with third-party service providers (such as online tutoring platforms) if necessary to provide tutoring services. Legal Requirements: Personal information may be disclosed if required by law or to protect the safety of the learner (e.g., to child protection authorities).

7.2 Confidentiality

Tutors are required to maintain strict confidentiality of all personal and educational information provided by learners and their families. Personal information will only be disclosed with the express consent of the learner or their guardian, except in cases where disclosure is legally required.

8. Protection of Personal Information

8.1 Security Measures

StudySphere implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or destruction. These measures may include: Encryption: Encrypting sensitive data, especially when stored electronically or transmitted via email. Password Protection: Ensuring that all devices used to store personal information are protected by strong, regularly updated passwords. Access Control: Limiting access to personal information to only those individuals (e.g., the tutor) who require it for legitimate purposes.

8.2 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that compromises personal information, StudySphere will take immediate steps to contain the breach and notify affected individuals and the relevant authorities in compliance with POPIA.

9. Access to and Correction of Personal Information

9.1 Right to Access

Learners or their guardians have the right to request access to the personal information that StudySphere holds about them. This request will be processed within a reasonable time, subject to any legal or administrative restrictions.

9.2 Right to Correction

If learners or guardians believe that any personal information held is incorrect or incomplete, they have the right to request corrections. StudySphere will promptly correct any inaccuracies upon verification.

10. Data Transfer Outside South Africa

StudySphere will not transfer personal information outside of South Africa unless adequate safeguards are in place to protect the information, in compliance with POPIA. In cases where information is transferred internationally (e.g., through international online tutoring platforms), consent will be obtained from the learner or their guardian.

11. Use of Online Platforms and Tools

11.1 Online Tutoring

If tutoring services are provided online, StudySphere will ensure that the platforms used comply with data protection standards, including secure communication protocols. Tutors must inform learners and guardians of the risks involved in online tutoring and the measures taken to protect their data.

11.2 Data Tracking and Analytics

If any data tracking or analytics tools (e.g., website cookies or learning management systems) are used, StudySphere will inform users and obtain consent where necessary.

12. Complaints and Queries

Any questions, concerns, or complaints regarding the handling of personal information should be directed to StudySphere at studyshere@gmail.com. Complaints will be investigated promptly, and corrective actions will be taken where necessary to ensure compliance with this privacy policy and applicable laws.

13. Updates to this Policy

StudySphere reserves the right to amend or update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated to learners and their guardians via email or other communication channels.
